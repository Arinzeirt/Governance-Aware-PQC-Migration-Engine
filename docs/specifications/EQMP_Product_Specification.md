# 1. Vision

To establish EQMP as the leading governance-aware platform for enterprise post-quantum cryptographic migration, enabling organisations to transition to quantum-resistant security through informed governance, operational intelligence, and evidence-based decision support.

---

# 2. Mission

EQMP empowers organisations to discover cryptographic assets, assess post-quantum readiness, prioritise migration activities, and execute governance-led cryptographic transformation while maintaining business continuity.

---

# 3. Product Philosophy

EQMP is not a vulnerability scanner.

EQMP is an enterprise governance platform that supports executive decision-making throughout the post-quantum migration lifecycle.

Every capability within EQMP must assist organisations in answering a governance question, understanding available evidence, and determining an appropriate course of action.

---

# 4. Product Principles

The following principles govern every future release of EQMP.

## Principle 1

Governance before technology.

## Principle 2

Every workspace supports one executive decision.

## Principle 3

Evidence before recommendation.

## Principle 4

Recommendations must support business continuity.

## Principle 5

Cryptographic agility is an organisational capability.

## Principle 6

Research must translate into operational value.

# 6. Enterprise Workspaces

EQMP consists of dedicated enterprise workspaces.

## Executive Workspace

Provides enterprise-level post-quantum readiness visibility and governance recommendations.

## Assessment Workspace

Discovers enterprise cryptographic assets and executes readiness assessments.

## Inventory Workspace

Maintains the enterprise cryptographic inventory and migration priorities.

## Repository Explorer

Analyses software repositories to identify cryptographic dependencies.

## Migration Planner

Supports governance-led migration planning and implementation.

## Reports

Generates executive and technical reports.

## Research Centre

Provides access to research notes, governance frameworks, publications, and supporting evidence.

# 7. Governance Experience (GX)

4. Recommendation
5. Recommended Next Step

This Governance Experience forms the core interaction philosophy of EQMP.
